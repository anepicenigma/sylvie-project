Hello!

I'm Sylvie Stojanovski.

I am an artist, creative facilitator and community organizer currently based in Scarborough, Canada.

I live by the motto: Do what you love, love what you do.

For me, that means… creating intuitive artwork, designing workshops that promote transformative healing and connection, and exploring the world with a deep sense of curiosity and wonder.

When I’m not making creative work or travelling, I enjoy eating spicy pad thai, cycling in outdoor spaces, reading self-help books, making vision boards, and laughing whole-heartedly.

If you have an idea for a creative collaboration, I would love to chat!

Drop me a line at sylviestojanovski@gmail.com or just fill out the form here!
